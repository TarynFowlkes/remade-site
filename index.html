The problem

We don't have a waste problem.
We have a coordination problem.

Valuable industrial materials go to landfill every day — not because there's no demand for them, but because there's no infrastructure connecting the two sides.

Waste producers pay significant costs to dispose of materials that makers and remanufacturers would pay to receive. Both sides lose. The landfill wins.

"One company's waste is another's raw material. ReMade connects the two — then sells valuable reimagined products."

LA County's C&D diversion requirements are already in force — 65% diversion mandated now, rising to 70% for mixed C&D and 100% for vegetation and soil. For waste producers, compliance documentation is becoming as urgent as cost reduction. ReMade's automated ESG reporting transforms platform adoption from a cost optimization decision into an operational necessity.

The platform

The innovation is not any single technology — it is the integration of all five stages into one system. Today, waste diversion, materials sourcing, logistics, ESG reporting, and product sales each happen in separate systems, or not at all. ReMade closes the loop.

01
List
Waste Intake Tool
Waste producers upload material listings capturing technical specs — material grade, volume, frequency, and weight. Makers submit requirements through the Demand Intake Tool, creating a structured, searchable demand profile on the other side of the marketplace.
02
Match
Smart Match Engine
The proprietary Smart Match Engine algorithmically pairs available materials with the most profitable and sustainable downstream use — surfacing connections that manual sourcing would never find at scale. Trade secrets protect the core weighting logic.
03
Move
Integrated logistics
An integrated logistics layer coordinates material transfer between waste producers and makers — managing hauler connections and delivery scheduling within the platform. No external broker required.
04
Measure
Automated ESG reporting
Automated ESG reporting generates waste diversion metrics and emissions data directly from transaction records, without manual inputs. Compliance-grade reporting for regulators, investors, and corporate sustainability teams — auditable, not estimated.
05
Sell
Integrated marketplace
Makers list finished products made from reclaimed materials on the integrated marketplace, creating direct-to-consumer and B2B sales channels. The value loop closes entirely within the system — from disposal listing to finished product sale.

Environmental impact

Landfill diversion

Every successful match diverts material from landfill, reducing methane and energy from processing and burial. The platform quantifies diversion at the transaction level — auditable and reportable, not estimated.

Emissions reduction

Diverting materials reduces Scope 3 emissions associated with disposal, and lowers demand for virgin raw material extraction and manufacturing — cutting energy consumption across the supply chain.

Resource efficiency

Reclaimed materials entering a new product lifecycle extend the useful life of existing resources, reducing overall consumption of natural materials and moving the economy closer to a true closed-loop model.
